Fine Arts Library Laptop Loaning Program

  • Students may borrow laptops from the Fine Arts Service Desk with a valid Cornell ID.
  • Laptop loan period is three hours, or until half an hour before closing.
  • Laptops may only be used in Fine Arts Library.
  • Laptop batteries may be exchanged for fully-charged batteries as needed.
  • NO OVERNIGHT LOAN OF LAPTOPS

Late Fee:

  • $.04 per minute/per item
  • $35.00 maximum late fee per item
  • No grace period

Peripherals:
(available upon request)

  • DVD/CD-ROM Drive
  • Headphones


Renewal Policy:
Laptops may be renewed at the Service Desk.

Patron Responsibilities:

  • DO NOT LEAVE LAPTOPS UNATTENDED.
  • Patrons are responsible for any equipment lost or damaged.
  • Upon return, computers will be checked to ensure all equipment is intact.
  • Patrons must return all peripherals borrowed with laptop.

Damage/Replacement Cost: $2,200

Applications include:

  • Microsoft Office 2000 Professional
  • Windows XP Professional SP2
  • Symantec Antivirus 9.0
  • Internet Explorer 6.0 SP2
  • Mozilla 1.7.3
  • SpyBot Search and Destroy 1.3
  • Macromedia Flash Player 7.0
  • Bear Access 2003 Lab Version
  • Windows Media Player 10

  • TurboZip Express 2.0

IMPORTANT! Saving your work:
If you wish to save something temporarily on the laptop, use the desktop folder named "My Documents in Thawspace." Material saved in any other location on the hard drive or desktop WILL BE LOST if the computer is shut down or loses power for any reason. Please save your work early and often.

If you wish to save your work to a more permanent location, you may do so by:

  • Saving to your personal diskette, CD, or USB flash memory drive
  • E-mailing your work as an attachment
  • File transfer (FTP) to your CU PEOPLE account or other server space


Please note: Any work left in the "Thawspace" folder when the laptop is returned will be available to the next patron. Be sure to delete any personal work you don't want others to see. "Thawspace" material is cleared out every 48 hours.

Printing:
You can print from the laptop using your Net-Print account. There are two Net-Print printers in the Fine Arts Library Main Reading Room.
