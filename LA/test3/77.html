Video/DVD Collection

Africana Library's video and DVD collection are documentaries focusing on the Black experience in America and the disapora. Most of the locally produced video tapes document on-campus lectures and conference panel sessions.

Our video and DVD collection does not circulate outside of the Cornell Unversity community.
