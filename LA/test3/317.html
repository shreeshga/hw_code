Classroom complements

"Cornell's libraries truly complement the classroom. With course reserves of textbooks, solution manuals, TakeNotes and supplementary readings on hold, the library has effectively lightened my backpack while enabling me to still study to the fullest potential.

Research help

Research help pages on the Library's Web site are the perfect place to start -- or finish -- a project. The Introduction to Research page can help you define a topic, and the search tips help you find sources.

Print collection

“The sheer ridiculousness of the number of books at the Library really strikes me as amazing. Look for anything from an Irish epic to Japanese history to Greek etymology, and you’re going to find an entire shelf of books on the exact same topic, half of which are going to be better than the one you thought you were looking for.

Manga collection

"Kroch has a great manga selection. Some of my favorite manga is there, and it helps me to learn Japanese, too. The Asia libraries have books in so many other languages -- I use Japanese and Chinese for my studies and just to read."

Jenny Shen, undergraduate, majoring in history

 

Olin's DVD collection

"I love taking out DVDs from the bottom floor of Olin -- whenever I want to watch a movie, I just go there. I especially like the foreign film selection and loads of interesting documentaries, as well as comedies and TV shows. In the winter, I probably go at least once a week or once every two weeks.

News aggregator databases

"In addition to Library PressDisplay, there are thousands of newspapers available online, but they can be too spread out to find easily. We've put together a Web page of the major online newspaper sites, the News Aggregator Databases, to make it easier and faster to find newspapers online."

Library PressDisplay

"Imagine a news kiosk in Paris. Then BE there, with the digital news service Library PressDisplay. It's page images of today's issue of a current newspaper (plus a 60-day archive) for 700 newspapers from 76 countries in 38 languages.

Hospitality Insight blog

"The blog helps people stay up to date on all the news in the hospitality world — career resources, industry news and more."

- Morgan Miller '10, student worker in the Néstle Library

Olin Library Copy Center

"The Olin Library Copy Center has made my graduate school experience so much easier! I am usually running between campus and my home in Fall Creek, so the ability to email papers and articles from anywhere and pick them up at Olin saves me tremendous amounts of time. Plus Olin offers the best copy rates on campus — 3 cents a copy for larger documents.
