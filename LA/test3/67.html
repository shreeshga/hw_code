Africana Library Microform Collection

The library has over 18,000 microforms focusing primarily on the American Civil Rights and Black Power Movements. Included are the papers of various African American organizations, and presidential papers of the Roosevelt, Truman, Kennedy and Johnson administrations relating to race relations.
