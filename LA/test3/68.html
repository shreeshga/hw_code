Purchase Request: This form is used for requesting an item(s) to be purchased by the Africana Library. If you have not done so, please check the library catalog to see if the item you need is available at any of the Cornell Libraries. If the title you requested cannot be ordered because it is not within the scope of the Africana Library collection, your request will be forwarded to the librarian responsible for selecting in the subject area.
