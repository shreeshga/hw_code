Extensive resources

"As someone who listens to 'Fresh Air' on NPR, what I love about the library is that every time I hear an author or a book that I'm interested in, I can get it right away -- either here in the library, or from Borrow Direct or Interlibrary Loan. The library provides immediate gratification."

- Barbara Berger Eden, Director of Preservation and Collection Management

Olin Media Center

"Did you know that you can take microforms, microfilm, microfiche and microprint and read, print, scan or e-mail them to yourself -- all from Olin's Media Center? We offer photomicrographic machines that can creat PDFs directly, and printing costs money but the scanning is free."

- Suzanne Schwartz, Olin Media Student Supervisor

The library mindset

"I enjoy the Library because I'm able to get away from my desk and my computer, which is huge. To focus my energies on independent tasks and my research in a different environment gives me a totally different frame of mind."

- Alisa Royem, second-year graduate student in hydrology and water resources

Finding aids

"Using our Web site, researchers can find large amounts of catalog information, including our lists of thousands of vintage films. Finding aids describe and list the contents of a collection and provide an easy way to search for old Cornell games, for example."

- Evan Earle '02, special collections assistant

Study spaces in Kroch Asia

“It’s incredibly quiet and really warm. The individual seating is nice and the café is quite convenient (except during the lunch hour!) Studying into the night, it’s so nice to have a place to fill up your caffeine levels and get some sugar into your bloodstream.”

Christine Xu, undergraduate, CALS

The Library Annex

"Getting materials sent from the Annex really doesn't take long. If you have something you want that our library has, it's faster than Borrow Direct or Interlibrary Loan; it'll be here within a couple of days, and you can pick it up at whatever library you want it delivered to."
